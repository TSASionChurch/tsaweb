SAY Group: Empowering Youth Since 2008

A Legacy of Faith and Service

By John Doe | October 12, 2024

Since its formation in 2008, the SAY Group has been a vital part of the Salvation Army Church Tamil Sion, focusing on empowering youth through community service and spiritual growth. Over the years, we have organized various initiatives, including youth retreats, medical camps, and community service projects.

Youth Retreats and Spiritual Growth

Our youth retreats offer a chance for young members to deepen their faith and connect with one another. These retreats not only provide spiritual nourishment but also foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie among participants.

Community Service Initiatives

The SAY Group actively participates in community service, organizing events such as medical camps that provide essential healthcare services to those in need. Our commitment to serving others reflects the core values of our faith and strengthens our community ties.

Celebrating Traditions

Every year, the SAY Group takes pride in leading the caroling festivities during the holiday season. Our carols not only spread joy but also bring together our congregation and the wider community. Additionally, we create beautiful cribs that depict the nativity story, allowing us to express our creativity and faith.

Looking Ahead

As we move forward, the SAY Group remains committed to our mission of service, fellowship, and spiritual growth. We invite all young people to join us in our efforts to make a positive impact in our community. Together, we can continue to uplift lives and spread love.

Get Involved!

If you are interested in becoming part of our dynamic group, please join us every Tuesday at 6 PM at the church. We would love to have you with us as we work together to serve and grow in faith!
