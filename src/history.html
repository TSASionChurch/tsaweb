Our History

1940: The Beginning
1940
Ministry Starts in Dharavi Slums
In 1940, our ministry began its journey in the heart of the Dharavi slums, reaching out to those in need.
1960s: Home Worship
1960s
Salvationists Gather at Home
During the 1960s, worship transitioned into homes, fostering a close-knit community spirit among believers.
1970s: Corps Officer Granted
1970s
Establishment of Corps Officer
In the 1970s, the leadership structure strengthened with the granting of corps officer positions to better serve the community.
1980s: Formation of TSA Sion Corps
1980s
TSA Sion Corps Church Established
The 1980s saw the birth of the TSA Sion Corps church, expanding our outreach and community involvement.
Present: Service and Outreach
2024
Expanding Our Mission
Today, we continue to expand our services and outreach, dedicated to doing God's work and serving our community.

About Us

The Salvation Army Church Tamil Sion is committed to serving our community through faith, worship, and outreach programs.
