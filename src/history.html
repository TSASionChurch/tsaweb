Our History

The Salvation Army Church Tamil Sion has a rich history that dates back to our founding in the early 20th century. Our mission has always been to serve our community through faith, worship, and humanitarian efforts. Over the decades, we have grown from a small gathering into a vibrant church family dedicated to uplifting lives.

In the 1950s, our church became a focal point for the Tamil community in Mumbai, providing spiritual guidance and support to those in need. We have hosted numerous outreach programs, including food drives, education initiatives, and community service events that reflect our commitment to making a difference.

Today, we continue to thrive as a place of worship and community engagement, welcoming individuals and families from all walks of life. Our doors are always open, and we invite you to join us in our journey of faith and service.
